We collect personal information from you, including information about your:

  • contact information
  • computer or network
  • interactions with us
  • or purchase information

We collect your personal information to:

  1. make a reservation for your dining
  2. contact you if a mistake happens
  3. contact you when finding a lost property

Besides our staff, we share this information with:

  • the police officer to look for the owner of a valuable product.
  • authorities with permission to assisst legal related activities

Providing some information is optional. If you choose not to enter your phone number, we'll be unable to inform you of accidents, like double booking a table.
We keep your information safe by storing it in encrypted files and only allow certain staff to access it.
We keep your information for two years at which point we securely destroy it by securely erasing all digital data.
You have the right to ask for a copy of any personal information we hold about you, and to ask for it to be corrected if you think it is wrong.
If you’d like to ask for a copy of your information or to have it corrected, please contact us at thehaukaurestaurant@gmail.com, or 09 401 4019, or 1 Island Street, Kerikeri, Bay of Islands.
